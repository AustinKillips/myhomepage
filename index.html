About

Austin Killips used to race bicycles professionally. She has set all time records on prestiguous ultra-distance bikepacking routes, stood on international and domestic podiums, and generated many pretty lines with biometric sensors. She also caused quite a stir after becoming first trans woman to win 'The Tour of the Gila'.

In the summer of twenty twenty four she decided to retire from cycling when the universe offered a chance to close the self destructive feedback loop that professional pedaling had become.

Austin now spends her time writing and making images that are both static and mobile with a small cohort of designers at Studio 405 in Providence, RI

Her interests include biometric data collection & self surveillance, Eric Rohmer, primary colors, Sisyphus, being-towards-death, and chance encounters with strangers.
